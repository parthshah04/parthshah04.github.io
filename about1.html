Welcome to White House Hostel

FACILITIES

  • The hostels are nestled amidst the beautiful Aravalli range, great potential and support for trekking, nature trails and adventure sports activities at the doorstep.
  • All rooms furnished with required furniture and with earth-air cooling, laundry facility and house-keeping services.
  • Each room is positioned to provide a beautiful view of the surrounding Aravalli Hills.
  • Wi-Fi enabled campus provides access to University's On-Line Library and Information Resource Centre within the hostel rooms.
  • Facilities to support vibrant social and recreational activities such as Yoga Centre, Forest Walk, Music Room, Dance Centre, Art & Craft Centre, Theatre Club, Photography Club, Adventure Club and Debating Society are provided, within close proximity of hostels.
  • Separate hostel facilities for girls and boys
  • All girls' hostels are single-bed accommodation with common toilets.
  • Boys hostel have a combination of single/double/triple bed accommodation with common toilets.
  • RO Water and Water Cooler are available in each wing of all floors of hostels.
  • Microwave ovens are provided in each hostel for warming food.
  • There are common rooms on each floor of the hostels, with TV.
  • Coffee/tea/soup vending machines are provided in hostels.
  • A female security guard and lady warden ensure that NU girl students are safe in the hostels.
